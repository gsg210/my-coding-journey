Week 1 Continued: Roadblocks

Today's class was... humbling, to say the least.

Our first activity covered relative paths, of which the first three I breezed right through. Couldn't figure out the fourth (and last) one for the life of me. Fortunately, we got the solution so I'll need to follow up on that.

Our next activities covered floating and positioning, both of which were quite the challenge. I thought I'd do just fine once I went hands-on with it, but no such luck.

Just so the concepts would stay fresh(ish) in my mind, I headed straight to Starbucks to plug away at our basic assignment. It took about 2 hours, but I made it through. Some parts of it took longer than others, of course, but I'm hoping that the more I incorporate into my homework and practice, the more proficient I'll get.

The recommended assignment is a beast. I started the basics of it, and ended up toiling for hours over the front page. It still needs quite a few tweaks.

Fortunately, we were introduced to CodePen, which allows you to work with HTML, CSS and JavaScript side-by-side so you can see your changes in real-time. Cool stuff.

Looking forward to getting the hang of this more. And I'm planning to meet up with classmates on Friday to go over concepts and homework.
